Disclaimers

If you do not know what this tool does, you should not be using it. If you do not know how to do what this tool does yourself with paper and a scientific calculator, you should not be using it as part of actually dosing the drugs mentioned.

This tool is just an automated formula and is not a substitute for a detailed theoretical and clinical knowledge of the relevant areas of pharmacy practice. Aminoglycosides are perfectly capable of killing people. This tool does not have any understanding of that, so you must.
